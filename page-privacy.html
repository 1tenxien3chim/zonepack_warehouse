II. Your rights as a data subject

Every data subject has the following rights:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to Erasure “Right to be Forgotten” (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR).

You may object to the processing of personal data for advertising purposes, including an analysis of customer data for advertising purposes, at any time without giving reasons.

Furthermore, the data subject also has a general right to object (see Art. 21 s. 1 GDPR). In this case, the objection to data processing must be reasoned. If the data processing is based on consent, your consent can be revoked at any time with future effect.

III. Processing of personal data

In the following, I would like to give you an overview of how the protection of your personal data is ensured when accessing this website and what types of personal data are processed for what purposes and to what extent.

1. Processing of data when accessing this website – log files

When accessing this website, information of a general nature is collected automatically. This information (server log files) includes, for example, the type of web browser, the operating system used, the domain name of your internet service provider and similar information. In addition, your IP address is transmitted and utilised in order to access the service you have requested. This information is necessary for technical purposes in order to correctly deliver the contents of the websites requested by you and is required for use of the Internet.

This log file data is anonymised or deleted immediately after the end of use. The legal basis for this data processing is Art. 6 s. 1 (f) GDPR.

2. Processing of data when using the website - your inquiries

If you send me an inquiry by e-mail, we will collect the data you provide for the purpose of processing and responding to your request. We will store this information for a period of up to 2 years for verification purposes. The legal basis for this data processing is Art. 6 s. 1 (f) GDPR.

3. E-mail newsletter subscription

We use a so-called double-opt-in procedure for newsletter registration. This means that after you have provided your e-mail address, a confirmation e-mail will be sent to the e-mail address you have provided, in which we will ask you to confirm that you wish to receive the newsletter. If you do not confirm this within 6 hours, your registration will be deleted automatically. Provided you have confirmed your wish to receive the newsletter, we will store your e-mail address until you unsubscribe. The storage of your e-mail address serves the purpose of being able to send you the newsletter. Furthermore, we save the time of your registration in order to prevent misuse of your personal data, and so that we can provide proof of correct sending. The legal basis for this processing of your personal data is Art. 6 s. 1 (a) GDPR.

The only mandatory information for sending the newsletter is the e-mail address. You have the right to revoke your consent to receive the newsletter at any time. Your revocation will not affect the lawfulness of the processing of your personal data until revocation has taken place. You can submit your revocation by clicking on the link provided in every newsletter e-mail, or by sending a message to the contact details provided on the website.

4. Use of the quick layout tool

At https://schnellauslegung.schuetz.net we offer you the possibility to carry out a virtual design of your individual panel heating/cooling system using software provided by us free of charge. To use the quick layout tool, it is necessary to register an account. The registration of your account takes place via a registration form. It is necessary to enter your e-mail address so that we can create a user account for you and know to which user the account is assigned. Further information can be provided voluntarily.

Data processing for the purpose of using the quick layout tool is carried out in accordance with Art. 6 s. 1 (b) GDPR, as a contractual relationship between you and us is created when you register your account and accept the terms of use. We delete the accounts and the associated data at the latest after ... years of inactivity.

5. Request via the watchlist

On our website you have the possibility to save products you are interested in on a watchlist for the duration of your session. You can then send us an inquiry about the saved products. We will process the data you provide for the purpose of processing and responding to your product inquiry. We store your inquiry and the associated data for [two] years for verification purposes. The legal basis for processing your data is Art. 6 s. 1 (b) GDPR.

6. Ticket Service

Through our ticket service, you can order the collection of IBC online on our website. After registering for this service and providing your company's data, you can, after we have assigned you a customer number, order online the collection of a corresponding container. You are obliged to provide the relevant information, otherwise we will not be able to collect the container. We store your order for a period of 3 years and then archive it until the statutory retention periods expire. After expiry of the statutory retention periods, your data will be deleted.  The legal basis for the processing of your data is the existing contractual relationship with you according to Art. 6 s. 1 (b) GDPR.

7. Reach measurement using etracker

On our website, we use a tool for reach measurement developed by our service provider etracker GmbH, Erste Brunnenstraße 1, 20459 Hamburg, Germany. This tool does not use cookies unless you have explicitly agreed to the use of cookies. Without cookies, we use the tool from etracker to analyse the statistical usage of our website so that we can structure and optimise it to meet your needs. For this purpose, we collect data about how and what you use on our website; this data is anonymised after 24 hours at the latest and aggregated into our statistics.
The legal basis for the processing of your data is Art. 6 para. 1 p. 1 lit. f of the DSGVO. Our legitimate interest lies in the design and optimisation of our website to meet our users’ demand. To opt out of reach measurement with etracker, click here.
